CableTech Wairarapa

CableTech, based in the Wairarapa, provides structured cabling solutions to distribute the Internet and media around your home or business premises.

The convenience of having the Internet available in your home or business premises where you want it and need it. Cables work for computers, especially gaming computers and business-critical computers, Smart TVs for streaming shows, and for Wireless Access Points. Wireless Access Points (WAPs) ensure that WiFi connectivity is provided around a home or business premises with minimal blank spots.
